Summary

Summary

• The lecture focused on using the telephone effectively in business settings.

• It outlined the dos and don'ts of phone etiquette to create a positive impression.

• Emphasized answering promptly, greeting politely, giving full attention, and resolving issues promptly.

• Highlighted the importance of note-taking, avoiding annoying habits like eating while on calls, and ending conversations positively.

• Live Answering: Emphasized the importance of live answers over voicemail on primary office lines.

• Prompt Response: Urged prompt answers and resolution of calls to maintain efficiency.

• Responsibility and Kindness: Encouraged taking responsibility for calls, offering help, and displaying kindness.

• Return Calls: Stressed the need to return phone messages within one working day.

• Professionalism: Highlighted the significance of a kind and professional demeanor during calls.

• Exceptional Service: Advocated going the extra mile to provide exceptional customer service.

What I learned

I Learned....

• Answering calls promptly within 3 rings and maintaining a friendly tone is crucial.

• Introducing oneself at the start of the call and actively listening to customers.

• Taking concise notes, avoiding distracting habits, and resolving issues promptly are key takeaways.

• Ending calls positively and following up on promises are essential for customer satisfaction.

• The significance of live answering and prompt responses.

• Taking responsibility for calls and providing courteous service.

• The importance of returning calls promptly and maintaining professionalism.

How, I can apply them

How can i apply them..

• Implementing the rule of answering calls within 3 rings and using a friendly tone.

• Introducing myself clearly at the start of each call and actively listening without distractions.

• Taking quick and relevant notes during conversations and summarizing them back to the caller.

• Avoiding eating or engaging in distracting habits during phone conversations and aiming to resolve issues promptly and positively.

• Prioritize live answering and prompt responses to calls in my workplace.

• Take responsibility for addressing queries rather than transferring excessively.

• Ensure timely return of calls/messages and maintain a professional demeanor during conversations.

• Strive to exceed expectations by providing exceptional service to callers and customers.
