Summary

Summary

• Job interviews are conversations between an applicant and an organization representative to exchange information.

• Effective communication, both verbal and non-verbal, plays a crucial role in interview success.

• Understanding the company, preparing answers, and showcasing active listening skills are vital.

• The STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) helps structure responses effectively.

• Dressing appropriately, organizing certificates, grooming, and maintaining professionalism are key.

What I learned

What I learned

• The importance of research before an interview, focusing on the company's background and job requirements.

• Preparing responses to common questions using the STAR method for clear and concise communication.

• The significance of professional attire, grooming, and non-verbal communication in making a positive impression.

• Organizing certificates and presenting them chronologically in a professional folder for easy reference.

• Dos and Don'ts during interviews, such as being punctual, avoiding personal oversharing, and showcasing enthusiasm.

How, I can apply them

How, I can apply them

• Research the company thoroughly before any interview to tailor responses effectively.

• Practice answering common questions using the STAR method to structure my responses clearly.

• Choose appropriate attire and focus on non-verbal cues like body language and maintaining professionalism.

• Organize my certificates in a professional folder, labeling and categorizing them for easy access during interviews.

• Implement dos and avoid don'ts during interviews to create a positive and professional impression.
